SOAR Leader

The primary goal of a SOAR Leader is to help facilitate the transition of undergraduate, graduate, transfer, and international students to Illinois Institute of Technology. SOAR Leaders will serve as positive role models for incoming students by facilitating group discussions on salient topics, sharing their student experiences, helping them connect to campus resources, answering questions about Illinois Tech, and helping them to acclimate to life as an Illinois Tech student.

FYE Mentor

First-year mentors lead small groups incoming students and introduce them to useful resources at Illinois Tech. New students participate in discussions and events with their group during the First-Year Seminar, may meet with their first-year mentors on an individual basis, and are able to participate in organizing group activities throughout their first year.
